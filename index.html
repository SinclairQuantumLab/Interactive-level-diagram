    How To Use

    • `Shared Diagrams` is the online catalog. You can browse public diagrams and sign in by email to manage your own YAML uploads.
    • `Local Diagrams` reads YAML files from a folder on this computer. If you clone or download this repo, `webapp/diagrams` is just another local folder you can choose.
    • The selected diagram and current layout are remembered. Reloading re-reads the YAML from the active source.
    • Click a fine-state bar to expand or collapse hyperfine structure.
    • Click a hyperfine bar to expand or collapse Zeeman sublevels.
    • Right-click any state to pin a movable information panel.
    • Turn on Hide Tool to click states, labels, or transitions and toggle whether they stay visible in the normal view.
    • Turn on Measure Tool to pick two visible state bars and compare their level spacings.
    • Drag pinned panels by their headers to reposition them.
    • Use your browser's zoom in/out to scale the surrounding UI and panel sizes, and use the mouse wheel or trackpad on the diagram to zoom the dashboard and atomic components themselves.
    • Use the magnetic-field panel to toggle Zeeman shifts and adjust their visual scale.
