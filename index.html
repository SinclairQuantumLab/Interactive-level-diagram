      How To Use

      • When this app is hosted as a website, built-in diagrams from the site's bundled `diagrams/` catalog load automatically.
      • If you want to work from your own local files instead, click `Choose local diagrams/ folder` and pick the `diagrams` folder on your computer. After that, the browser remembers the folder choice.
      • The selected diagram and current layout are remembered. Reloading re-reads the YAML from the active source, whether that is the built-in site catalog or your remembered local folder.
      • Click a fine-state bar to expand or collapse hyperfine structure.
      • Click a hyperfine bar to expand or collapse Zeeman sublevels.
      • Right-click any state to pin a movable information panel.
      • Turn on Hide Tool to click states, labels, or transitions and toggle whether they stay visible in the normal view.
      • Turn on Measure Tool to pick two visible state bars and compare their level spacings.
      • Drag pinned panels by their headers and use the mouse wheel or trackpad to zoom.
      • Use the magnetic-field panel to toggle Zeeman shifts and adjust their visual scale.
